Privacy Policy

How this site handles your data — plainly explained

This policy applies to ryanbishop.co.uk, operated by Ryan Bishop, an AI strategy consultant based in Surrey, UK. It explains what data this site collects, why, and what that means for you.

Last updated: February 2026

What this site collects

This site uses Google Analytics 4 in cookieless mode. That means:

  • No cookies are stored on your device
  • No persistent identifier is assigned to you
  • Your IP address is anonymised before it is processed — only your approximate location (country and region) is retained
  • Usage data such as pages visited, time on page, and traffic source is collected in aggregate

This site does not collect names, email addresses, or any information that directly identifies you as an individual unless you choose to contact me directly via email or LinkedIn.

Why this data is collected

Anonymised analytics data helps me understand which content is useful, where visitors come from, and how the site is being used. This informs future improvements to the site and supports the development of my consultancy practice.

Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, the legal basis for this analytics processing is legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)). Given that no cookies are set, no personal data is stored on your device, and IP addresses are anonymised before processing, this use is proportionate and unlikely to override your interests or rights.

Who processes your data

Analytics data is processed by Google LLC on my behalf, acting as a data processor. Google Analytics is configured to anonymise IP addresses and disable client-side storage. For more information on how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

I do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any other third party.

How long data is retained

Aggregated analytics data is retained for 14 months in Google Analytics, in line with Google's default data retention settings. Because no personal identifiers are stored, there is no individual-level data to delete.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Be informed — which this policy fulfils
  • Object to processing — you can opt out of Google Analytics across all sites using Google's opt-out browser add-on
  • Access, rectification, and erasure — as no personally identifiable data is held about you by this site, these rights are not applicable in practice. If you have contacted me directly, you may request that I delete your correspondence.

External links

This site contains links to LinkedIn and other external platforms. Once you leave ryanbishop.co.uk, this privacy policy no longer applies. I encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Changes to this policy

If the way this site handles data changes materially, this policy will be updated and the date at the top of this page will reflect that.
